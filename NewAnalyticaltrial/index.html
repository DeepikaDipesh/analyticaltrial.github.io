Reserach Question:

How much does people from different parts of the world are satisfied with their life and how does their income, number of their children and their country contribute to it? This will help the user to know the secret of healthy life in each country

Analysis

This new visualisation gives an overview of the data all together using parallel co-ordinates which gives user an underlying story of the research question.

Using multiple brush technique, user can select any country and any year to get information. For example below image shows that in India, though people are attained good education their scale of income is low and that makes them bit less unsatisfied with life.

With multibrush, User can select multiple countries for comparision. Thus below image shows that Turkey and Peru being a more democratic country, its citizen are more satisfied in life with less scale of income

Again between year 2010-2014 in mexico being the democratic country, the country's people are more satisfied added they have less number of children on average between 2 or 3.

In China over time, due to increase in democracy and scale of income, people have attained high education and achieved higher satisfaction in life but over time they have reduce their leisure and health life.

Conclusion:

Thus from above visualisation, it is confirmed that people statisfaction of life is more related to scale of income and their country play a major role. Other variables are dependent on these variables. Better satisfaction improves the health of individual.
